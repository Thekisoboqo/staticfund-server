## THE PROBLEM

South African households struggle with rising electricity costs. Prepaid electricity doesn't last the month, and people don't know which appliances are draining their money. When they try to go solar, installers use one-size-fits-all solutions — either too small (fails) or too big (too expensive). **Nobody knows their actual energy consumption.**

---

## OUR SOLUTION: STATICFUND

StaticFund is an **AI-powered energy audit app** that helps households understand and reduce their electricity costs.

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 📱 **Scan** | Point your phone camera at any appliance — AI identifies it instantly |
| 📊 **Audit** | Get complete energy consumption profile of your home |
| ☀️ **Size** | Calculate exact solar panel and battery size needed |
| 💡 **Tips** | Receive personalized energy-saving recommendations |
| 🔗 **Connect** | Find verified solar installers near you |

**Business Model:** Free for households. Solar installers pay commission per lead.
