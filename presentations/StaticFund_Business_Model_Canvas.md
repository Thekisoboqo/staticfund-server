# COMPARISON: All 3 Models

| Aspect | Canvas #1: Commission | Canvas #2: Subscription | Canvas #3: Transaction Fee |
|--------|----------------------|------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Revenue Type** | Per lead | Monthly recurring | Per sale |
| **Example Revenue** | R500 per lead | R500-3,000/month | 2-5% of sale |
| **Revenue on R100k sale** | R500 (one lead) | R500-3,000 (monthly) | R2,000-5,000 |
| **Predictability** | Medium | High | Low |
| **Installer Risk** | Low | Medium | Lowest |
| **Implementation** | Simple | Medium | Complex |
| **Best For** | Starting out | Scaling up | High-volume market |

---

# RECOMMENDED APPROACH

**Start with Canvas #1 (Commission):**
- Easiest to implement
- Low barrier for installer onboarding
- Proves the model works

**Scale to Canvas #2 (Subscription):**
- Once you have proven demand
- When installers see value
- For predictable revenue

**Consider Canvas #3 (Transaction Fee):**
- For high-value partnerships
- When you can track sales
- For premium installer relationships
