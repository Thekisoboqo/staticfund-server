## FULL TRANSCRIPT

---

### HOOK (10 seconds)

> "What if you could point your phone at any appliance and instantly know exactly how much electricity it's costing you — and how to stop it?"

---

### THE PROBLEM (25 seconds)

> "South African households are struggling with rising electricity costs. Prepaid electricity doesn't last the month. Families are frustrated, but they don't know which appliances are draining their money.
> 
> And when they try to go solar? Installers give them cookie-cutter solutions that either fail because they're too small, or cost way too much because they're oversized.
> 
> The real problem? Nobody knows their actual energy consumption."

---

### THE SOLUTION (30 seconds)

> "We built StaticFund to solve this.
> 
> StaticFund is an AI-powered energy audit app. You scan any appliance with your phone camera — our AI instantly recognizes it and tells you its power consumption.
> 
> Scan all your appliances, and you get a complete energy audit of your home.
> 
> We give you personalized tips to reduce your electricity bill. And if you're considering solar, we calculate the EXACT battery and panel size you need — not a guess.
> 
> For households, it's completely free. We make money by connecting users with solar installers who pay for qualified leads."

---

### TRACTION (30 seconds)

> "Here's what we've accomplished:
> 
> During this Summer Action Challenge, we built the entire StaticFund app from scratch — the AI scanning feature, the energy audit, the solar sizing calculator, and the saving tips.
> 
> We launched in December 2025. We have 8 users signed up and 14 appliances scanned.
> 
> We attended the NPS ESTEMIE NPO EV Workshop, where I pitched StaticFund to attendees. We're now in a group chat with them, and they're ready to try the app once our servers scale up.
> 
> We created a demo video, and we're actively building partnerships with solar installers."

---

### THE ASK & CLOSE (25 seconds)

> "We're looking for three things:
> 
> Team members — especially in marketing and business development.
> 
> Funding — to scale our servers and cover our AI API costs.
> 
> And most importantly — customers. Households who want to save electricity, and solar installers who want qualified leads.
> 
> I'm Thekiso Boqo, my co-founder is Kal-el Peterson, and we are StaticFund — Smart Energy Audits for a Sustainable Future.
> 
> Thank you."

## KEY TIPS FOR ELEVATOR PITCH

1. **Start strong** — the hook grabs attention immediately
2. **Be confident** — you built something real, show it
3. **Focus on traction** — what you've DONE matters most
4. **Clear ask** — tell them exactly what you need
5. **End with your name** — make it memorable

---

## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Stand up** while recording (more energy in your voice)
2. **Look directly at the camera**
3. **Speak with passion** — you're solving a real problem
4. **Keep a steady pace** — don't rush
5. **Practice 5+ times** before final recording
