## SLIDE 1: Hook / Title

## SLIDE 2: The Problem

**Title:** The Problem

**Bullet Points:**
- 💸 Rising electricity costs — prepaid doesn't last
- ❓ Households don't know which appliances drain their money
- 🔧 Solar installers give one-size-fits-all solutions
  - Too small → System fails
  - Too big → Too expensive

**Key Message:**
> "Nobody knows their actual energy consumption"

> **Design Tips:**
> - Red/orange warning colors
> - Use icons for each point
> - 3-4 bullet points maximum
