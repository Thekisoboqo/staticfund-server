## Design Notes for PowerPoint

### Color Scheme
| Element | Color |
|---------|-------|
| Primary (Headers) | Lions Red `#C8102E` |
| Accent (Highlights) | Gold `#FFD700` |
| Background | Black `#0A0A0A` or Dark Gray `#1A1A1A` |
| Text | White `#FFFFFF` |

### Fonts
- **Titles**: Bebas Neue or Impact (bold, sports feel)
- **Body**: Inter, Calibri, or Arial

### Tips
- Use full-bleed photos as slide backgrounds
- Add subtle red/gold gradient overlays on images
- Keep text minimal - let photos speak
- Use the Lions logo where appropriate
